EXPERIMENTS

Clock Harmonizer A clock where three hands play continuous harmony on a 12-tone circle.
? Twelve tones of the chromatic scale are mapped to the positions of a clock face. The three hands (hour, minute, second) move continuously to weave ever-changing harmonic flows.
Digital Clock Synthesizer A synth that converts clock numbers (hh:mm:ss) into real-time Just Intonation frequencies.
? The active digital clock display (hh:mm:ss) is converted into integer ratio frequencies, generating chords based on Just Intonation in real time. Try playing at -100x speed!
Circle of Fifths Visualizer A visualizer where the Circle of Fifths scale glows based on analyzed frequencies of uploaded audio.
? Upload an audio file to analyze its frequency components in real time, lighting up the corresponding keys on the Circle of Fifths dial. Works best with monophonic audio.
Essay: Keys and Their Images Music theory columns exploring the specific color and imagery evoked by different keys and scales.
? An essay examining the historical and sensory qualities associated with major/minor scales and keys, analyzing why they evoke specific feelings.
